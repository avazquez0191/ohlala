Next to the emblematic Paseo del Prado, and a few steps from the Central Park, Ohlala Café Bistro offers a light, fresh concept here in Havana City, combining aged notes with alluring flavors and textures created for a fun space. A bar where friends from all over the world come together, seduced by the music of a vibrant red piano that livens the days and bewitches the nights. The menu is inspired by a compilation of “cravings” out of simple recipes from around the world, emphasizing both the Cuban seasons, flavors and ingredients, and the eclectic notes and techniques of the French cuisine. The main objective of Ohlala Bistro Café is to provide a place that highlights the architecture of Old Havana, transforming it into a creative space that celebrates art in a cup of coffee, a cocktail, a crêpe brûleé… perhaps adding some magic threads of caramel or the red notes of a beautiful melody to complete an explosive pairing you will never forget.

Eclectic recipes and flavors with French cuisine techniques.

OUR CHEFF

Our staff is made up of local talent who have been trained to value the technique and classic aspects of the French cuisine, highlighting food flavors through the creation of simple but inviting recipes. Our purpose is to emphasize the typical preparations in a creative and eclectic context, by adapting them to original versions of age-old recipes. Our menus bring together different dishes from around the world. But each of them has the distinctive seal of Ohlala Café Bistro!
